# Netflix-EDA-Analysis

**Project Overview**

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Netflix's content catalog to uncover trends in movies and TV shows. The analysis focuses on content distribution, genre popularity, release trends, country-wise contributions, ratings, and content growth over time to provide insights into Netflix's content strategy.

**Objectives**

 - Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows.
 - Identify the most common genres on Netflix.
 - Explore content trends across years.
 - Examine country-wise content contributions.
 - Analyze content ratings and audience categories.
 - Discover patterns in Netflix's content expansion.

**Tools and Technologies**

 - Python
 - Pandas
 - NumPy
 - Matplotlib
 - Seaborn
 - Jupyter Notebook

**Dataset Information**

The dataset contains Netflix content details, including:

 - Title
 - Type (Movie / TV Show)
 - Director
 - Cast
 - Country
 - Date Added
 - Release Year
 - Rating
 - Duration
 - Genre (Listed In)

**Key Analysis**

 - Movies vs TV Shows Distribution
 - Content Growth by Year
 - Top Contributing Countries
 - Most Popular Genres
 - Content Ratings Analysis
 - Duration Analysis
 - Year-wise Release Trends
 - Missing Value Analysis and Data Cleaning

**Results**

 - Movies account for the majority of Netflix content.
 - Content additions increased significantly after 2015.
 - The United States contributes the highest volume of content.
 - Drama, International Movies, and Comedy are among the most popular genres.
 - Most content is targeted toward mature and teen audiences.
 - Netflix has steadily expanded its global content library over the years.
